Generate a compact listing pack.

Required fields:
- product name
- 3 key specs
- target scenario
- claim boundaries (what cannot be claimed)

Output sections:
1) Thai-friendly searchable title (give CN + EN transliteration-ready variant)
2) 5 bullets (benefit first, then spec)
3) FAQ x4 (size/material/usage/returns)
4) Claim-safe version (replace risky wording)

Rules:
- No medical/safety absolute claims
- Avoid exaggerated superlatives
- Keep each bullet <=28 Chinese chars
- Return in checklist style, no long paragraphs.
